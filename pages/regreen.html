Challenges and Problems

Challenge #1

Lack of knowledge in proper recycling guidelines, especially in terms of food waste

Challenge #2

Lack of motivation to properly carry out food recycling practices.

Challenge #3

Lack of support from the community if no one is carrying out food recycling practices.

Solution #1

Allow users to scan an item and instruct them how to recycle

Solution #2

Encourage actions to recycle through rewards

Solution #3

Build an online community and eventually encourage people to join

Hi-Fi Prototpe

Key Feature #1

Scanning your food waste and learn how to recycle through an interactive feature

Goal:

Instruct the user how to correctly recycle leftover food and fain leaf points.

Steps:

  1. Scan the food waste.
  2. Wait for the system to process your item.
  3. A clipart of your item shows up.
  4. Drag it into the correct bin to receive leaf points.

Key Feature #2

Growing your tree through recycling, and eventually plant a real tree as a reward.

Goal:

Motivate action of recycling by stimulating the process of watering a tree, and make a real impact by exchanging leaf points for planting a real tree.

Steps:

  1. Click on the water bubble of leaf points to water your sprout
  2. Continue to grow your tree through recycling.
  3. Keep track of your recycling history.
  4. Exchange your leaf points for rewards.

Key Feature #3

Compete with your friends and your community through the ranking system, and share your thought by posting online.

Goal:

Improve awareness to recycle through competing with other people, and generate a discussion forum for people to connect.

Steps:

  1. Gain a leaf by recycling.
  2. Check your status in Rank.
  3. Post your thoughts in Community.
  4. Like and Comment on others' posts.
